About Me

Who am I?

My name is Lagbaja Tamedo and I'm a mental health blogger. I realized that hundreds of thousands of Canadian international students like go through similar struggles and challenges when it comes to settling in the new environment, excelling in school, working to make ends meet, and so on. So I decided to create a platform where we can all share our mental struggles and know that "Hey, you are not alone in this".
